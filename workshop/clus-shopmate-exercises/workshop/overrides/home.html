AI PODs In One Minute

A Cisco AI POD is a repeatable infrastructure pattern for AI workloads: GPU compute, high-speed networking, storage, platform software, and operations tooling working together. In this lab, the GPUs are the center of the story. ShopMate Sports creates demand, NVIDIA NIM serves the model, GPUs accelerate the work, and Splunk helps you prove what happened.

Your path is simple: start a baseline collector, point the agent app at it, find the app trace, change the collector file to add GPU and NIM metrics, then compare the before/after evidence with the environment filter.
