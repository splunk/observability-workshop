# .conf26 Abstract Draft

## Session Title

Automated Resolution, Accelerated Insights: AI Remediation Agents in Splunk Observability Cloud

## Primary Abstract

AI is now part of the customer experience, which means AI failures are no longer isolated technical issues. They are customer trust issues. In this interactive session, we will show how Splunk Observability Cloud can help teams move from detecting a degraded digital experience to making a safe, governed remediation decision. Using an AI-powered claims portal scenario, we begin with what the customer sees first: browser slowdown, failed interactions, and frustration visible through RUM and Digital Experience Analytics, with Session Replay only when available. We then connect that experience to the affected business transaction and backend services, use AI-assisted investigation to assemble the evidence, and hand that context to a bounded remediation flow. The session focuses on the real challenge: not whether AI can suggest an action, but whether teams can trust, govern, observe, approve, execute, and validate that action in production. Attendees will leave with a practical model for human-in-the-loop AI remediation with clear guardrails and auditability.

## Short Backup Version

As AI moves into customer-facing workflows, observability teams need more than faster detection. They need a trustworthy path from evidence to action. In this interactive session, we will show how Splunk Observability Cloud helps teams start with real user impact through RUM and Digital Experience Analytics when available, connect that experience to the affected business transaction and service path, and support a governed remediation workflow. Using an AI claims portal scenario, we will demonstrate how AI-assisted investigation, policy controls, human approval, and validation work together to make remediation safer, more observable, and more auditable.

## Positioning Notes

- Start from customer impact, not backend symptoms.
- Emphasize governed remediation, not autonomous everything.
- Keep the architecture credible: Splunk provides detection, evidence, and observability; a separate orchestrator and remediation agent handle action.
