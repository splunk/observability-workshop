{{% notice icon="user" style="orange" title="Persona" %}}

You are a **Database Administrator (DBA)** or an **SRE** who has just been told that "the database feels slow". You have access to Splunk Observability Cloud and need to find out where to begin.

> [!splunk] We are going to look at *Splunk Database Monitoring* — a unified view across **Microsoft SQL Server**, **Oracle Database**, and **PostgreSQL** that surfaces query performance, server metrics, and the applications that depend on each database. The goal of this chapter is to orient yourself: know what the product covers and how to navigate to it.
