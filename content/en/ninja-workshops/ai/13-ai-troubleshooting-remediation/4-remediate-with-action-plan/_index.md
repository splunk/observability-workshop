{{% notice icon="user" style="orange" title="Persona" %}}

You are the **service owner** responsible for restoring the affected system. You will use the AI remediation plan to choose a hypothesis, run guarded actions, and validate recovery.

> [!splunk] The action plan is a structured guide, not an automatic change executor. Your role is to review every suggested step, run only approved commands, submit outputs, and confirm that the alert condition is resolved.
