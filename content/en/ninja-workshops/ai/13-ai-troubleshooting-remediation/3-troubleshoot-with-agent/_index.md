You are an **incident commander** leading the first technical triage. Your job is to turn the alert page into a shared understanding of impact, likely cause, and confidence level.

> [!splunk] Splunk Observability Cloud automatically starts AI troubleshooting for supported alerts when users access the alert. You will review the generated analysis, inspect the evidence, and decide which hypothesis deserves remediation work.
