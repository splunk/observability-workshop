You have completed the **AI Troubleshooting Agent and Remediation Plan** workshop.

You practiced how to:

- Confirm whether an alert is supported for AI troubleshooting.
- Deploy and monitor the sample checkout app on local or cloud Kubernetes.
- Use the Overview, Root Cause Analysis, and Evidence tabs to evaluate suspected root causes.
- Open the AI-generated action plan and classify remediation steps by operational risk.
- Run guarded diagnostic steps, submit output, and validate recovery.
- Apply the workflow to advanced APM, Kubernetes, and cross-signal incident scenarios.
- Build readiness checks, runbook templates, and adoption metrics for production teams.

## Next Steps

Take these actions before rolling the workflow out broadly:

1. Identify the highest-value APM and Kubernetes alerts that meet the current support requirements.
2. Improve service ownership and metadata where evidence was hard to correlate.
3. Add the runbook template to your incident management process.
4. Run at least one tabletop exercise before using AI-generated remediation steps in production.
5. Track investigation quality and remediation safety alongside MTTR.
