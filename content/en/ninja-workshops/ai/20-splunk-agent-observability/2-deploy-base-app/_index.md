Time to get hands-on. In this chapter you'll log into your lab instance and stand up the
**uninstrumented** healthcare assistant. This gives you a working baseline, and a firsthand
look at the blind spot you're about to eliminate.

{{% notice title="Persona" style="orange" icon="user" %}}

As Careful Health Provider's **AI engineer**, your first task is to get the assistant running
exactly as it exists today: functional, but with **zero visibility** into the agent's
reasoning, quality, or safety. Everything you add next builds on this baseline.

{{% /notice %}}

> [!splunk] You'll deploy from the `1-base-app` folder. 
> There is **no Splunk Agent
> Observability code here yet**. That's intentional. This is the "before" picture you'll
> transform over the rest of the workshop.

Continue to the subsections to access your lab, configure the app, and run it.
