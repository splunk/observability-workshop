**Splunk Observability Cloud** integrates advanced AI and machine learning capabilities throughout the platform to help you work smarter, faster, and more efficiently. These AI-powered features help you identify issues faster, understand relationships better, and make data-driven decisions with confidence.

This workshop provides hands-on experience with the various AI and ML capabilities available in Splunk Observability Cloud, including:

* **Related Content**: Discover how AI surfaces contextually relevant dashboards, alerts, and resources as you navigate through your observability data.
* **AutoDetect**: Learn how machine learning automatically creates intelligent detectors that adapt to your environment's unique patterns and behaviors.
* **Tag Spotlight**: Explore how AI-powered analytics help you quickly identify the root cause of performance issues by analyzing patterns across your tags and metadata.
* **Log Observer AI**: See how AI enhances log analysis with intelligent pattern detection, anomaly identification, and natural language insights.
* **APM AI Assistant**: Experience AI-driven guidance for troubleshooting application performance issues and understanding complex traces.
* **Predictive Analytics**: Understand how ML models forecast future trends and help you proactively address potential issues before they impact users.

By the end of this workshop, you'll understand how to effectively use AI capabilities in Splunk Observability Cloud to:

- Reduce mean time to detection (MTTD) and mean time to resolution (MTTR)
- Automatically discover anomalies and unusual patterns
- Navigate complex relationships between services, infrastructure, and logs
- Make informed decisions based on AI-powered insights
